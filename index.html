About Me

Amy Ciuffoletti is someone who is excited to learn and try new things. When Covid hit and remote teaching and learning became the new normal, she was ready! Already utilizing digital instruction in her classroom, she took it upon herself to help her colleagues adopt tools and resources to aid in remote instruction. This lead to Amy taking on greater leadership in educational technology by assisting her school in learning the Schoology Learning Management System when it was implemented in the fall of 2020. When blended learning was implemented, Amy found it to be an inspiring challenge that resulted in engaging digital lessons which still continue in a normal educational setting. Amy's interest in digital learning and software only increased, resulting in pursuing an additional degree in Software Development beginning in Fall of 2021, which lead to her participation in the TechWise pilot program in March of 2022. As her studies progress, Amy continues to fall more and more in love with this new path, especially creating interactive programs and creative problem-solving. In addition to learning, Amy loves the outdoors, travel, music, and comedy. She currently lives in Pittsburgh, PA with her husband Doug and amazing step-daughter Lillian.
