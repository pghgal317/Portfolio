TechWise

In spring of 2022, I had the amazing opportunity to join the first cohort of TechWise! Techwise is a joint venture between TalentSprint(India) and Google(US), along with 5 colleges / universities in the United States. This intensive study in software engineering has been such a tremendous opportunity. I can't even begin to quantify how much I have learned during my time in this program! Here are some highlights.

Fizz-Buzz

Please excuse me while I geek out for a moment. As a fan of numbers and numerical patterns, one of my favorite programming challenges we solved was Fizz-Buzz. Throughout our first course, we solved this problem multiple ways using Python. Here is my favorite way to solve Fizz-Buzz in 4 lines of code.

Fibonacci Sequence

Another numerical pattern that I really enjoyed exploring was Leonardo Pisano Bigollo's Fibonacci Sequence! It was fun using multiple ways to solve these problems!

Menu Demo

After the completion of TechWise Course 1, we broke into groups to create different projects. My group worked on creating a fully-functional chess game. One of my tasks involved creating the game menu, and here is the demo! This code was organized using Object-Oriented Programming which was challenging and a big accomplishment for my group! What an incredible learning opportunity!
